SECTION 3 — sample-size-gates + wilson-intervals 
(priority 2, do together)
====================================================
Add Wilson score confidence intervals to the track record block 
in both daily and weekly email reports.

Wilson score formula for proportion p, n trials, z=1.96:
  center = (p + z²/2n) / (1 + z²/n)
  margin = z * sqrt(p(1-p)/n + z²/4n²) / (1 + z²/n)
  low = center - margin
  high = center + margin

Where to add it:
In reporter.py (or equivalent), find where Win Rate is computed 
and printed in the TRACK RECORD block. After the win rate line, 
add a new line:
  "  95% CI:         {low:.1%} – {high:.1%}  (n={n})"

Edge cases:
  - n=0: print "  95% CI:         N/A (no resolved signals)"
  - n<5: print "  95% CI:         {low:.1%} – {high:.1%}  
    (n={n}, low confidence)"

Also add the same CI line to the Research Probe Track Record 
block using its own n and win rate.

Do NOT change any other part of the report format.
Prove by: unit test with known values (p=0.5, n=10 should give 
CI roughly 0.237–0.763). pytest exits 0.

Mark sample-size-gates and wilson-intervals as "done" in 
backlog.json
